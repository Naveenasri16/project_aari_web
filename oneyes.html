AARI BLOUSES IN INDIA

Aari blouses are a popular traditional garment in India, known for their intricate embroidery work.

Aari work is an embroidery technique originating from the Indian subcontinent, popular in both India and Pakistan. Aari work blouse involves the creation of a pattern through the use of techniques such as couching, sequins and beadwork.

Aari work blouses are made with an age-old technique of Indian embroidery that involves the stitching designs on fabric using a needle and a hook at the end.

Aari work on blouses is done by putting the fabric in a frame and doing the embroidery using crochet needles.
